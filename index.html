PCe System

Personal Cognitive Environment

PCe is the hardware and operating environment designed specifically for local-first cognition. It’s where your machine stops being “a PC” and becomes a dedicated cognitive instrument for you and your AI.

Tuned hardware + stack Local-first, privacy-anchored Built for Umbra-Noesis stack

What is the PCe System?

The PCe System (Personal Cognitive Environment) is the physical and logical home for your Umbra-Noesis stack. It defines how storage, GPUs, memory, and the OS are arranged to prioritize:

  • Low-latency local AI inference as a first-class workload.
  • Resilient storage layouts for long-term cognitive archives.
  • Predictable performance across multi-node deployments.

Roles inside the ecosystem

PCe is where the Umbra-Noesis concepts become metal: Genesis, MobileCore, PCe_FE, future racks and nodes. Each PCe profile is a documented, repeatable build that can be deployed, shipped, or licensed.

Founders / Lab

High-end nodes for development, deep testing, and long-horizon experiments.

Field / Mobile

MobileCore-style builds for working from tugs, trucks, or remote cabins.

Client / PCe Kits

Future turnkey systems or blueprints derived from the same architecture.

PCe Roadmap (High Level)

  • PCe_FE Prototype — internal reference build, used as the baseline Personal Cognitive Environment.
  • Blueprints & Profiles — documented configurations for home labs, field rigs, and racks.
  • PCe Product Line — future kits, reference builds, or licensed designs derived from Umbra-Noesis.

Integration Points

PCe is designed to host:

  • Umbra-Noesis OS (UmbraOS) as the base environment.
  • EVAA Cognition as the resident local AI layer.
  • E.V.E. Systems and training stacks as optional modules.
